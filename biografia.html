Emma Wihanto

Multidisciplinary creative working across visual arts, photography and digital media.
Rooted in a lifelong relationship with art, my practice blends analog and digital processes to explore form, emotion and aesthetics. I work primarily with acrylics and mixed media, alongside photography and fashion design. Recently, my focus has expanded into 3D modeling, motion graphics and video editing, incorporating tools like Blender, Photoshop and Premiere Pro into my daily workflow. I move fluidly between tactile and digital worlds - always seeking new ways to translate feelings and ideas into form.
